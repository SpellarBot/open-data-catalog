(NASGA). Our host hotel in downtown Portland is the Doubletree by Hilton, with a rate of
only $79/night, an excellent rate for a full-service hotel in a major city.


Heres how the schedule works:
Sunday, January 27  Opening reception, board/committee meetings.
Monday, January 28  All-day tour, including farm, processing, nursery, and research sites.
Tuesday January 29 and Wednesday, January 30  Joint Conference of NARBA and NASGA, with joint plenary sessions and several tracks of educational breakouts.

The dates weve chosen will also allow you to take in two other great events:
