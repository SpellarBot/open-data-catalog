## Description

The Annual Publication is a proposed program of road and structure projects that are planned for current fiscal year. They are the roadway, spot, structure, engineering, and land acquisition files. The Land Acquisition Layer File is populated by selecting project records containing a Land Acquisition Improvement Type.
