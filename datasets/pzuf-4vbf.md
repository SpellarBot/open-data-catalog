## Description

Certified models meet all ENERGY STAR requirements as listed in the Version 1.0 ENERGY STAR Program Requirements for Small Networking Equipment that are effective as of September 3, 2013. A detailed listing of key efficiency criteria are available at https://www.energystar.gov/index.cfm?c=small_network_equipment.pr_crit_small_network_equipment
