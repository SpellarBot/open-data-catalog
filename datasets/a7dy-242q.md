## Description

This layer displays all of the currently adopted Iowa Highway segments as related to the Adopt-A-Highway program. These layers were developed for utilization by the Electronic Permitting System (EPS) at the Iowa Department of Transportation. This layer is updated every hour at the top of the hour.?
