## Description

This dataset is up-to-date but newer better data can be retrieved at: https://data.energystar.gov/dataset/ENERGY-STAR-Certified-Displays/xsyb-v8gs

Certified models meet all ENERGY STAR requirements as listed in the Version 7.0 ENERGY STAR Program Requirements for Displays that are effective as of July 1, 2016. A detailed listing of key efficiency criteria are available at https://www.energystar.gov/index.cfm?c=monitors.pr_crit_monitors.
